Centre for Postgraduates Studies is dedicated to educating leaders who can think transformatively and create lasting value in the workplace and society. CPS offers a broad array of interdisciplinary technical graduate degrees, professional certificates, short courses, and custom programs that are taught by outstanding faculty from across campus along with professors of practice and experts from industry.

Courses and programs are delivered on Michigan's campus, online, at work locations around the world, or in a combination of delivery modes. Learn more about us.
